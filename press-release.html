The Duskbloods

Expected Release:2026

Platform(s):Nintendo Switch 2

Genre: Action RPG

The Duskbloods is an upcoming multiplayer action RPG developed by FromSoftware, directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki, and set to release in 2026 exclusively for the Nintendo Switch 2. This title marks a departure from FromSoftware's traditional single-player experiences, introducing a dynamic PvPvE (Player vs. Player vs. Environment) gameplay where up to eight players compete and cooperate in a gothic, vampire-inspired world.

FromSoftware continues to focus on these confirmed projects while promising to uphold the quality and innovation fans expect. For the latest official announcements, always refer to FromSoftware's official channels.
